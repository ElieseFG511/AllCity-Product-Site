Our Story

Created by Eric “Norm Will Rise” Rosenbaum in 2014 for his graffiti crew and members of his tattoo parlour in Los Angelas. He got asked about his bags and when he told people they were amazed. He decided to market to the people who were as adventurous as him. The company is looking to respect his wishes after passing in 2019 by branching out to handbags, messenger bags, and coats.

Our Product

“All City” is a new company that creates stylish bags for creatives and urban explorers. They create bags that are sleek, spacious and stylish that will give street cred while allowing for anonymity. They currently specialise durable and longlasting backpacks, from sustainable materials. This product is great for creatives because it allows them to take their supplies, no matter what it is, along with them and truly explore the environments they live within and find new inspiration.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can this backpack hold?

The bag can carry up to a weight of twenty pounds, holding around twenty-two cans of Ironlak spray paint, or more usefully, a laptop, a bunch of books, maybe some water and a camera.

-Norm

Are these bags made in Sweatshops?

They are not. We have taken the steps to ensure more than humane working conditions for our employees, every backpack is currently made in house from materials either recycled or crafted locally. We care for our community and those around the world, and we wish to take the steps to help abolish the idea of needing sweatshops in the industry.

-Norm

Will there be more vibrant colors?

More than likely not. We provide bags to those looking for anonymity, that does mean dulled colors in a lot of cases just to match the background of the landscape you are in. We do provide some accommodations if you wish to add some more style to the bag, however we do strictly work with toned-down hues.
